<!-- =================== ABOUT ME =================== -->
<div align="center"><h2>🧑‍💻 About Me</h2></div>

I'm **Prince Malani**, a software developer who builds **native Android applications** and the **backend systems** that power them, increasingly with **AI and LLMs** woven into the product layer. I completed my **Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology** from **LDRP Institute of Technology** in **2024**, and I've spent the time since shipping real, production-grade software end to end.

On the **Android** side, I work in **Kotlin** with a strong emphasis on **Clean Architecture**, **MVVM**, and a **single-activity** structure backed by the full **Jetpack** toolkit — building apps that stay testable and maintainable as they scale. On the **backend**, I design **REST and real-time WebSocket APIs** in **Python** and **Node.js**, with both **SQL** and **NoSQL** data layers behind them. More recently, my focus has shifted toward **AI application development** — **RAG pipelines**, **vector search**, and **LLM-powered features** built directly into shipping products.

What drives me is the engineering craft underneath the product: clean code, predictable systems, and the small architectural decisions that decide whether software ages well. I care about building things that are **reliable, readable, and ready to grow** — and I enjoy the full journey from a blank screen to a deployed feature in users' hands.
